Bangla WikiConnect is a collaborative initiative...

...focused on enhancing and expanding Wikipedia’s content in Bengali. Through a variety of engaging contests, edit-a-thons, and training programs, we aim to develop high-quality, inclusive content on Wikipedia and its sister projects such as Wikiquote, Wikivoyage, Wikibooks, and Wiktionary.
